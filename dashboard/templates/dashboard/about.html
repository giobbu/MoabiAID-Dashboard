    General information

    Brussels Mobi-AID (Brussels MOBIlity-Advanced Indicators Dashboard) aims to facilitate the visualisation and analysis of data related to the movement of trucks in the Brussels Capital Region (BCR). This should provide better insights into the dynamics of truck mobility in Brussels, that should inform policy makers on decisiosns regarding mobility. The ultimate goal would be to create a model that can be used to route truck traffic in real time and result in better mobility in the Brussels Capital Region.

    To achieve this, a dashboard was implemented that allows visitors to view the real-time traffic of trucks in BCR and to view charts and other insights for historical data. We also make predictions on the expected traffic situation in the future using Machine Learning models that we developed for this purpose. Future research and findings regarding this subject should also be made available through this platform.

    The Mobi-AID platform is developed by the Machine Learning Group (MLG) of the Université libre de Bruxelles (ULB). The truck data is provided by Bruxelles Mobilité and is annotated with additional data from Waze and other useful sources of open data on mobility.
